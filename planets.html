Solar System Planets 🪐

Explore all 8 planets in our Solar System with AI-powered learning.

Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun. It is the smallest planet in our Solar System. A year on Mercury lasts only 88 Earth days.

Venus

Venus is the hottest planet in the Solar System. Its thick atmosphere traps heat. It is often called Earth's twin.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun. It is the only known planet that supports life. About 71% of Earth is covered by water.

Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet. Scientists believe water once existed on Mars. It is one of the most explored planets.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System. It has more than 90 moons. The Great Red Spot is a giant storm on Jupiter.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its beautiful rings. It is the second largest planet. Its rings are made of ice and rock particles.

Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side unlike other planets. It appears blue-green due to methane gas. It is the seventh planet from the Sun.

Neptune

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun. It has the fastest winds in the Solar System. It appears deep blue in color.
